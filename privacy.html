Privacy Policy - NovelGrimoire

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

We collect basic device information to ensure app compatibility and improve performance:

  • Android version and device model
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Screen resolution and display settings

1.2 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience:

  • Reading progress and bookmarks (stored locally)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Session duration and frequency

1.3 Content Data

All novel content is accessed from third-party sources. We do not:

  • Store novel content on our servers
  • Control or modify third-party content
  • Claim ownership of any novel content

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Personalize your reading experience
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to add new features

2.2 Analytics

We use anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with the app:

  • Most used features and sources
  • Reading habits and preferences
  • Device performance metrics
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • Reading progress, bookmarks, and settings remain on your device
  • Downloaded novels are stored locally for offline reading

3.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable backup features:

  • Data is encrypted before transmission
  • Only app settings and reading progress are backed up
  • No novel content is uploaded to our servers

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Ad Networks

We may use ad networks to monetize the app:

  • Ads are shown based on general interests
  • No personal information is shared with advertisers
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in device settings

4.2 Novel Sources

The app connects to third-party novel websites:

  • We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices
  • Each source has its own terms and privacy policy
  • We recommend reviewing their policies individually

5. Data Rights

5.1 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored about you
  • Delete your data and app settings
  • Export your reading progress and bookmarks
  • Opt out of analytics collection

5.2 Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Clear app data in device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us for additional data removal requests

6. Children's Privacy

NovelGrimoire is not intended for children under 13. We do not:

  • Knowingly collect information from children
  • Target content to minors
  • Provide child-specific features

7. International Users

The app is available globally. Please note:

  • Data is processed according to this privacy policy
  • Some content may not be available in all regions
  • Local laws may apply to content access

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy:

  • When adding new features or services
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To improve clarity and transparency
  • Changes will be communicated in the app

9. Copyright and Content

9.1 Content Ownership

Important legal information:

  • All novel content belongs to original authors and publishers
  • We do not claim ownership of any novel content
  • The app is a reader tool, not a content provider

9.2 DMCA Compliance

We respect intellectual property rights:

  • We respond to valid DMCA takedown notices
  • Copyright owners can report infringing content
  • We will remove access to reported content when appropriate

By using NovelGrimoire, you:

  • Understand that content is provided by third parties
  • Acknowledge that we are not responsible for content legality
